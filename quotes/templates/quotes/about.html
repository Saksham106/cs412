About This Project

This web application displays random quotes from Socrates. Each time you visit the main page, you’ll see a randomly chosen Socrates quote along with an image of him.

About Socrates

Socrates (469 – 399 BCE) was a classical Greek philosopher, often considered the founder of Western philosophy. He is best known for his method of questioning—now called the Socratic Method—which encouraged critical thinking and dialogue. Though he left no writings of his own, his ideas were recorded by students like Plato. Socrates emphasized the pursuit of virtue and self-knowledge, famously declaring that “the unexamined life is not worth living.”
